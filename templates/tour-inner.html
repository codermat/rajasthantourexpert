Detailed Itinerary

Day 01: Delhi

Welcome to India! On your arrival at the Delhi international airport or your desired location you will be welcomed and assisted by our representative or chauffeur and after take guided tour of Delhi In Old Delhi visit the Raj Ghat - the cremation sites of Mahatma Gandhi , Jama Masjid and drive past Red Fort and Chandni Chowk. In New Delhi visit the Qtub Minar built by Qutub-ud-Din Aibek in 1199, Humayun's Tomb, India Gate and Lakshminarayan Temple. Also drive past President's House, Parliament House and Government Secretariat Buildings. Overnight Delhi.
